Information We Collect

We collect information when you create an account, enroll in courses, purchase products, or contact us.

  • Name, email, and phone number
  • Account login credentials
  • Course enrollments and progress
  • Billing and shipping information
  • Purchase and download history
  • Artwork uploads and submissions
  • Device, browser, and usage data
  • Cookies and analytics information

How We Use Information

We use your information to operate our courses, marketplace, and improve your overall experience.

  • Manage accounts and course access
  • Deliver online classes and digital content
  • Process orders and shipments
  • Provide customer support
  • Personalize learning and product recommendations
  • Send updates, offers, and notifications
  • Prevent fraud and secure transactions

Marketplace & Orders

When you buy products, art kits, or digital downloads, we collect transaction details necessary for payment, shipping, and delivery. We may share limited information with payment and logistics providers.

Children’s Privacy

Some courses are designed for children. Personal information for users under 18 must be provided by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

Artwork & Media

We may showcase student artwork, class projects, photos, and videos for educational, promotional, or portfolio purposes. Consent will be obtained where required.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with trusted service providers such as payment gateways, shipping partners, hosting providers, and online class platforms.

Cookies

Cookies help us remember preferences, maintain shopping carts, analyze traffic, and improve your browsing experience.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online system is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal information, and opt out of promotional communications at any time.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
